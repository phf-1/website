* TODO Exercises are discussion subjects

Each exercise may be subject to discussions. They have a "discuss" button that
pre-fills an email, ready to be updated and sent. As a result, the recipient of the
message may respond. In this sense, the "exercise" answers back and may be error-
corrected or improved.

* TODO Data collected and stored in the browser.

When a user reviews an exercise, they may rate their own performance. The generated data
is stored in their browser and never transmitted to the server or anyone else.

* TODO Review algorithms execute in the browser.

The exercise scheduling algorithm uses the stored data to schedule exercises and maximize
learning efficiency.
